About Me

I've always had a deep curiosity for discovering how the world works. From disassembling things around the house as a child to building competition robots in college, my thirst for knowledge fueled my passion for the field of engineering. While working in a research laboratory at university, I took up the task of writing a program to automate data collection, management and analysis after noticing the grave inefficiencies in the lab workflow. This experience sparked my interest in programming, so I took the dive into software engineering and haven't looked back since.

I have worked extensively with Ruby on Rails, React, Redux, Express and Node.js, developing several production websites including a full-stack clone of the popular gaming voice chatting app Discord on the React-Rails stack and lead a team of developers to build a smart grocery management/meal-planning app on the MERN Stack (MongoDB, Express, React, Node) .

I love challenges and solving complex problems. As a software engineer, I strive to develop efficient and innovative solutions in order to optimize a user's experience and satisfaction. On top of all this, I enjoy learning and adopting new and upcoming changes in technology to enhance my skill set.
